Our Vision & Mission

We welcome you with great joy to PCEA Zimmerman Church, a vibrant community of believers committed to spiritual growth, fellowship, and service. Our doors are open to everyone seeking to know more about God's love and purpose for their lives.

"The name of the Lord is a strong tower; the righteous run into it and are safe." - Proverbs 18:10

Our mission is to share the transformative message of Jesus Christ, nurture disciples through biblical teaching, and serve our community with compassion and excellence. We strive to create an atmosphere where people can encounter God and grow in their faith journey.

Place of Worship

Our Ministries

Join one of our vibrant ministries and grow in faith and fellowship

PCMF

PCEA Men's Fellowship (PCMF) is dedicated to attracting, leading, and developing men in Christ. Our goal is to bring all men to a saving knowledge of Jesus Christ and equip them for godly leadership in their homes, church, and community.

Learn More

Woman's Guild

The Woman's Guild is a fellowship of Christian women committed to spiritual growth, service, and evangelism. Through Bible study, prayer, and community outreach, we seek to impact lives and glorify God in all we do.

Learn More

Youth Ministry

Our Youth Ministry (ZYF) provides a dynamic environment for young people to connect with God and each other. Through worship, teaching, and fellowship, we equip youth to live out their faith in today's world.
